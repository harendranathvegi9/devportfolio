Pamela DeButts

Front End Web Developer

Bio

Pamela is an entertainer turned developer. Being a very creative and sociable person, Pamela has spent the majority of her adult life in both show business and customer service.
In recent years, Pamela was looking for a career change that would give her the intellectual and creative stimulation that she craves. That is when she discovered web development.
Already being a Rutgers alumni, Pamela got accepted into the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp program. It is an intense, 24 week program that teaches students the skills needed to become a successful full stack web developer. In this program, she found a new creative passion, especially in front end web development.
Pamela has been able to apply her unique background and experience to her career as a developer. This experience involving customer service, art, comedy, psychology, marketing, public speaking and economics have all been invaluable to her work as a developer. She now would love to bring her skills and unique perspective to you.
